I'm a graduate student at the University of Washington pursuing my PhD in astronomy with a focus on data science. Currently, I'm working with observations of halo gas in the Milky Way to constrain the kinematics and structure of the circumgalactic medium and investigate how galactic-scale feedback mechanisms shape galaxies. More broadly, I'm interested in understanding the processes that drive galaxy evolution across cosmic time, from the high-redshift universe to the Milky Way. I'm also excited about exploring the ways data visualization can be applied to facilitate scientific insight and communication as astronomy enters the era of big data.
